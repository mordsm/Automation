The fastest way to start something .new

Sometimes you need to spin up a quick sandbox and dive right into coding. That's why we have these easy to remember shortcuts - type the relevant .new URL into your browser's address bar and get a sandbox instantly.
  • React: react.new
  • Vue: vue.new
  • Angular: ng.new
  • Vanilla JavaScript: js.new
  • TypeScript: ts.new
  • For everything else: csb.new

Happy coding!
